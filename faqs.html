Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

Ordering & Payment

How can I place an order?

+

You can place an order through our website by following these steps:

  1. Browse our collection and select your desired items
  2. Add items to your shopping cart
  3. Proceed to checkout
  4. Fill in your shipping and payment information
  5. Review and confirm your order

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept various payment methods including:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Bank Transfer
  • Buy Now Pay Later options (Affirm, Klarna)

Shipping & Delivery

How long will it take to receive my order?

+

Delivery times typically range from 3-7 business days for in-stock items. Custom orders may take 4-6 weeks. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.

Do you offer free shipping?

+

Yes, we offer free shipping on orders over $500 within the continental United States. For orders below $500, shipping costs will be calculated at checkout based on your location and the items purchased.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. The item must be in its original condition and packaging. Custom orders are non-returnable.

How do I initiate a return?

+

To initiate a return:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to your order history
  3. Select the item you wish to return
  4. Follow the return instructions

You can also contact our customer service team for assistance.

Product Information

Do you offer warranty on your products?

+

Yes, all our products come with a standard manufacturer's warranty. The warranty period varies by product:

  • Beds and Sofas: 5 years
  • Dining Sets: 3 years
  • Accessories: 1 year

Do you offer assembly services?

+

Yes, we offer professional assembly services for an additional fee. You can add this service during checkout. Our trained technicians will ensure your furniture is properly assembled and installed.
